Welcome to Original Gaming Australia!


These rules listed here must be followed at all times to ensure our players enjoy their exerpience.
Please take time to read the rules listed below and if you are in need of any assistance please contact staff either through ingame admin chat "@ help Throbbin Hood is calling me fat!" or the discord; "https://discord.gg/tCE2VNW".

1. Basic Rules

  • You will be permanently banned (without warning) for:
    • Prop spam. This is strictly off limits.
    • Doxing players. Doxing means to release another players personal information without their permission.
    • DDOS and DDOS threats are not taken lightly.
    • Using game exploits intentionally.
    • Money exploits or spawning money. Any accomplice to this will be banned aswell.
    • Cheating, macros, hacks etc.

  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Bullying and racism is not permitted.
  • Do not prop abuse. (Prop climb, prop push, etc).
  • You must attempt to keep all props frozen at all times to ensure good performance.
  • Do not rope spam.
  • Mic spam and sound emitter abuse are not allowed.
  • When casting a demotion, discuss why in /OOC first and give time for the person being demoted to respond.
    • Do not start a vote if objections are raised.
    • This can only be used for job abuses directly related to RP.
    • Eg: /OOC Demotion of Jeremy Salsa for FailRP and NLR any objections?
  • Do not attempt to find loopholes in the rules.
  • Staff decisions are final. If you have a problem with a staff members decision please go to our discord (link at top of page).

2. Roleplay Properly

    Basic RP Rules:
  • Do not RDM under any circumstances. You must have a valid reason to kill such as self defence, trespassing or
  • Follow your job rules.
  • Do not self supply. This means you cannot change to a shop class, spawn shipments and change back to your previous job straight after.
  • Do not metagame. This means you must pretend you don't know a hitman is a hitman.
    • Your gang is seen as a gang while you are breaking the law. This means that if your blood leader kills someone, the police can arrest a normal blood aswell.
  • You are ALWAYS in character. You cannot be an OOC job.
  • Use the /OOC properly. It is the only time you can speak out of character. Do not taunt in OOC.
  • Use /cr to report crimes to the police.
  • Government jobs are only allowed to own a printer if it's in a bank.(Banker job).
    • This is considered corrupt and the banker could get you arrested, or you could pay a bit extra ;)

    Weapons and Combat:
  • You must mark a KOS area clearly. Otherwise a clear verbal/chat warning must be given before the player is killed.
  • You can be instantly cuffed/searched/weapons confiscated/arrested if you are seen by the Government carrying any firearm in public.
  • One handed and melee weapons are legal by default. You can carry (but not wield) them in public.
  • Two handed weapons are illegal by default (you cannot carry them at all) unless otherwise stated on the law board.
    • A Gun License allows you to legally carry (but not wield) 2 handed weapons in public.
    • A Gun License allows you to legally wield 2 handed weapons within your establishment.
  • Do not put innocent lives at risk (bait cops by walking around with a gun out for no reason) nor act as a meat shield for others.
  • Try to minimise bunny hopping during combat.

    • Stealing:
  • You are only allowed to mug for $1000 or less. Do not repetitively mug a player.
  • You MUST advertise muggings. Example: "/advert (MUG) Give me $1000 or I'll kill you!"
  • You cannot steal items which are crucial for someones job, they may be destroyed however.
    • These items include meth cookware, cocaine barrels etc.
  • Stealing during an RP transaction is not allowed, regardless of the job you are playing as.
    • This includes stealing ammo or other items as someone spawns them.
  • For any transaction involving currency, the seller must provide said goods or a refund.

    • AFK and Suicide:
  • Do not AFK for long periods of time as any job other than Citizen or Hobo.
  • You are not immune to RP when you are AFK. (You can be pick pocketed, raided, etc.)
  • No AFK boxes. (Blocking yourself in so you can't be raided).
  • Do not suicide or change job to avoid RP situations.
    • We don't care if you suicide with friends for laughs, we're not that serious, just don't break any roleplay.

3. Roleplay Rules

New Life Rule (NLR):
  • If you die in public, you must not return to your point of death for 5 minutes.
  • If you die on your property, you may only return early if there is no raid or RP situation ongoing.
    • You must wait for any raids to complete before returning.
  • When you die, you forget all details about your past life. You remember nothing, apart from needing to avoid your area of death.
    • You are not allowed to rejoin combat or regain memory just because someone from your old life mentions the same issue in your new life.
  • A police officer cannot arrest a person that killed them, however if someone is wanted by the police, the officer may attempt to arrest them.
    • This goes to say the police officer who died cannot run directly towards the previous death, but may perhaps find the wanted person nearby. Play fairly.

    FearRP:
  • You must value your roleplay life. If someone has a gun to your head you must follow their orders.
  • When under FearRP, you may not draw a weapon on your attacker unless they turn away first.
  • Requests to the victim (demands) must be reasonable. Remember you must advertise the mug before you attempt to take their money.
    • You may order the victim to let you inside of their base.
  • Your gun must be pointed at the victim for FearRP to apply. Crowd control can be hard for one person.
    • If you turn around or stop threatening the victim at any point it will not be considered FailRP if he draws a gun and kills you.

    • Raiding:
  • NO RDM: Do not kill innocent bystanders unless they pose a direct threat to your raid or have been warned clearly to move away.
  • You must /advert Raid. If you are raiding with a buddy you must advert with their name in the advert.
    • Broad adverts (/ooc mug|raid|thief|counterraid|kidnap|hit) are not allowed. Adverts must be clear and specific.
    • It is Metagaming if people respond to your advert in-character. Eg: Arrest you prior to any actual raid, or run away, etc.
  • You must wait 5-10 seconds after advertising to commence your raid. You cannot kill any targets during this time either.
  • If you realise during the raid that the base is already being raided, you must leave instantly and /ooc Raid Cancelled/Over
  • Police are only considered a direct threat to the raid only if they are directly and knowingly observing it. (Weapons drawn etc)
  • You may not heal yourself (armors and health packs) during a raid.
  • If you die during a raid you cannot come back afterwards and keep helping with the raid.
  • You cannot steal items which are crucial for someone job, they may be destroyed however.
    • These items meth cookware, cocaine barrel etc.
  • You must /advert Raid Over when you have completed your raid and exited the building.
  • A group may only raid the same base 15 minutes after their previous raid was finished.
  • Raids can only last up to 10 minutes. Once a raid has reached this time limit, it is over and the raider must leave.
    • Do not shoot raiders who are leaving because of the 10 minute time limit.
    • Raiders cannot intentionally stall by calling raid then randomly roaming the streets for several minutes.
    • Raiders cannot intentionally stall if they have finished the raid. Leave and call it over.
  • Hitmen allowed to raid but only to kill their target. They are not allowed to steal.

    • Declaring War:
  • Both gangs can wage war between each other. The gangs can also wage war (one at a time) against the Government.
  • No gangs are at war by default. To start a war the gang leader must "/advert War against _____. Do you accept?"
  • Gangs must have 2 members (including the leader) to commence a war.
  • If the opposing leader declines or doesn't reply you cannot commence a war.
  • The Government cannot initiate a war, but the Mayor (or Police Chief if no Mayor) can accept a war declaration.
  • The war is over when all members of a gang/group are killed once each.
    • Once you have died during a war, you must attempt to avoid all conflict.
  • You must /advert War over once all members have been killed.
  • You cannot gang up in wars. Eg: Blood and Crip vs the Government.
  • Each group has their own 10 minute 'war cooldown' where they must wait before they can participate again.

    • Kidnapping:
  • Only two thieves working together or a gang with two members (including the leader) may kidnap.
  • Kidnappings can only last up to 10 minutes.
  • You may not kidnap citizens or jobs that work alone. (Hitmen, lone security guards, meth cooks etc.)
  • The maximum you can ransom someone for is $10,000.
  • The police must value the life of the victim and either mount a raid operation or pay the ransom.
  • If no one pays the ransom you may kill or release the victim.
  • You must /advert Kidnap before commencing a kidnapping and also /advert Kidnap over after it has been completed.
    • Once you advert a kidnap you are required to wait 10 seconds before commencing, just like raiding.
  • Don't forget to follow FearRP when being kidnapped.

    • 4. Base Building Rules

    General Base Rules:
  • Do not block roads or tunnels with anything, including toll booths.
  • Do not enclose NPC's for personal use. These must remain accessible to all players at all times.
  • Attempt to make your base as attractive as possible.
  • Only purchase a door if you intend to use the interior.
  • No one way materials or props. You may only use simple, two way windows.
  • Using nocollided props to walk through is not allowed.
  • Do not black box your base. (This means making the walls and the floor all the same, pure black colour so it's challenging to navigate.
  • You cannot build your base in a way where your valuables are destroyed if you get raided. Eg: Forcing a printer underwater.
  • Do not use contraints on your build.
    • Only constrain your build when you are ready to save. (Feel free to use Smart Weld)
    • When pasting with advanced dupe, ensure that it is "Without Constraints".
  • Players that are building may place a "Building" sign, disallowing people from raiding their base. Please note:
    • They must be actively building a base and have no valuables.
  • Signs are to be used for RP purposes only and may only be placed on or inside the owner's property.
    • An RP legitimate protest may spawn signs in public. It's a protest after all!
    • Screens are not allowed to float above a street. You must incorporate them into a sign if you want to advertise this way.

    Base Defences:
  • Base entrances must not be excessively difficult (or long) so you can simply camp it from the outside.
  • KOS tunnels must be wide enough for two people in all directions and must not force the raiders to crouch or jump. (Including world areas)
  • KOS tunnels (and pits) are not permitted to have any holes (faded or not) in the floor where raiders can inadvertently fall off.
  • World doors and fading doors are the only allowed areas of your base which can fit one person at a time.
  • You cannot use typical fence props (that can be shot through) as a part of your base defenses.
  • You cannot design your base in a way where you can run out a secret door and attack raiders from behind.
  • Players must not be required to break glass in order to get into your base.

    • Fading Door Rules:
  • Only two locked doors may be used in a base. A locked door is considered a locked world door, or a fading door.
    • The first door is for the primary entrance, and a second door may be used to defend valuables.
    • You have a maximum of 1 locked door in your entrance, regardless of how your entrance is designed.
    • Doors must be built with a single prop to ensure they can be lock picked. No overlapping props are allowed at doors.
    • All fading doors must be lockpickable and have an attached keypad/button directly next to the door, on both sides, which is clearly visible.
    • The keypad must stay open for at least 4 seconds once a successful password has been entered.
  • You may have 2 extra doors, however these doors can only be triggered by buttons which must be placed directly on the centre or side of the door.
  • All elevators and timed doors are considered a locked door. While you can use them, it's strategically far better that you don't.
  • You are not permitted to use any hotkeys or fast methods of access to get through your front door. You must use your keypad.
  • Fading doors are to be used as doors, windows and box lids only. Don't make bridges, stairs, floors or ceilings that fade.
  • Fading props cannot be the same material as its surroundings in an attempt to hide it. This applies to all fading props.
  • Fading car door entrances must stay open for at least 4 seconds, no matter the method used to fade.
  • You must not pulse, fade, use, or open any door that is being lock picked. Note: This does not apply to windows.
  • You are permitted to use custom doors (such as SteamID doors) for your valuable room only. The front door must be a normal type.

    • Fading Window Rules:
  • Fading windows must be positioned on the same level as the target area. They cannot be on a second story, for example.
    • Using any windows (fading or not) on the roof or floor is not permitted.
    • Only an open (no fading windows) ledge that overlooks the front of a base is permitted to be out of level with the target area.
  • Fading windows must stay open for at least 1 second once they are used. (Hold them open manually, toggle, wire timer, etc)
  • A fading window must look like a window. Eg: Glass, tinted, fence material, etc. They cannot be opaque or fully transparent.
  • Fading props cannot be the same material as its surroundings in an attempt to hide it. This applies to all fading props.
  • A fading window prop must be of a height where person's on both sides can see waist to head, whether armed or not.
    • This means a minimum window height of '05' when looking at the tooltip measurements of a plate or block.
    • (You can find these props by searching for '05' in the Q menu)

    Shop Rules:
  • Gun stores and other shops must be built within the following guidelines:
    • Wiremod automatic bases are permitted as long as you are not always AFK. You still need to roleplay.
    • Stores must have a front counter area where trading with customers takes place.
    • The shop owner must man the counter and also must be clearly visible to facilitate roleplay.
    • Stores without a front counter are not in the best interests of RP and will be removed.
  • The store itself should be primarily defended from inside the base. This means unmanned kiosks are not allowed.

    • Wiremod:
  • Using Wiremod entities for anything apart from their intended design is not tolerated.
    • A button is to be used as a button, a pixel is to be used as a pixel, etc.
  • You are not permitted to actively hide any buttons or keypads in a base which has valuables in it.
    • You must not use materials which blend them into the background.
    • You must not use colours or transparencies which blend them into the background.
    • You must not use tiny buttons/pads placed on areas which make them difficult to see.
    • You must not hide buttons under furniture, behind props, in small gaps, etc.
  • You are not permitted to build wire based gambling machines. (To the discretion of MG staff)

    • 5. Job Specifics

    Banker

    Description:

    • As a Banker, set up a shop and store items for people.
    • Rent out safety deposit boxes to customers.
    • Hire up to 2 security guards to help defend your establishment.

    Rules:

    • As a Banker, your Bank must be open to the public at all times. You are not allowed to AFK for over 5 minutes.
    • As a Banker, you cannot steal from money printers. Understand this is a bannable offence as it is considered FailRP.
    • Only the banker and his guards can defend the bank. Bystanders or customers cannot assist a banker defend his base.

    Tips:

    • Offer gangs special deals for storing their items. They can store their items in a private vault and reduce the need for them to have a base.
    • Banker can be a difficult role to master in following all the base rules while in turn building an effective base.

    Bloods & Blood Leader

    Description:

    • Organize raids against rival gangs and businesses.
    • Make money off the production and sale of drugs.
    • Protect your gang base.

    Rules:

    • Gangs are not allowed to be private or friends only.
      • You are allowed to keep un-trusted gang members out of your valuables room though.
    • Gangs need at least 2 members (including the Leader) to raid.
    • Gangs cannot raid without their leader; if the leader dies the raid is over.
    • Bloods are only permitted to have one base.
    • All gang raids must begin by the leader typing /advert Raid with gang to begin and /advert Raid over when it's over.
    • Also refer global raiding rules found in Section 3.

    Tips:

    • If you have a base of valuables, leave at least one person behind to scout for sneaky raiders.
    • Scout out establishments before you raid them. Wait for key opportunities such as the owners going to go get food.

    Citizen

    Description:

    • A Citizen is expected to follow the law.
    • They have no specific role in city life.

    Rules:

    • A maximum of two citizens can base together.

    Crips & Crip Leader

    Description:

    • Organize raids against rival gangs and businesses.
    • Make money off the production and sale of drugs.
    • Protect your gang base.

    Rules:

    • Gangs are not allowed to be private or friends only.
      • You are allowed to keep un-trusted gang members out of your valuables room though.
    • Gangs need at least 2 members (including the Leader) to raid.
    • Gangs cannot raid without their leader; if the leader dies the raid is over.
    • Crips are only permitted to have one base.
    • All gang raids must begin by the leader typing /ooc Raid to begin and /ooc Raid Over when it's over.
    • Also refer global raiding rules found in Section 3.

    Tips:

    • If you have a base of valuables, leave at least one person behind to scout for sneaky raiders.
    • Scout out establishments before you raid them. Wait for key opportunities such as the owners going to go get food.
    • You can have a maximum of 1 Hired Goon. They cannot assist in raids, but may defend the gang base in case of a raid.

    Donating Thief

    Description:

    • Break into shops and houses and steal things.
    • Pickpocket people for some extra cash.
    • This is a solo job, however they are allowed to base and raid with one other thief.

    Tips:

    • Click here to see Thief Rules

    Gun Dealer

    Description:

    • Supplies the city with weapons, armour and ammo.
    • Must create a shop and conduct transactions there.
    • Has a Gun License by default.
    • Consider hiring security.

    Rules:

    • As a Gun Dealer, you must sell weapons to the public from your own store. You may not base with any other shopkeepers.
    • As a Gun Dealer, you can only sell items from your store.
    • As a Gun Dealer, you may have a maximum of two Security Guards.

    Tips:

    • Consider hiring a security guard or two. Gun stores are high value targets and as such are often raided.
    • It is not illegal to sell weapons to someone who doesn't carry a license. It is up to the person holding a weapon to have a license for it.

    Goon

    Description:

    • Defend illegal stores and criminals bases for a fee.
    • Offer other random services to Criminals.

    Rules:

    • As a Goon, you can work for criminals only.
    • As a Goon, you cannot base with other Hired Goons.
    • As a Goon, you may not directly or indirectly assist with raids in any way.
      • You can however defend the base of your raiders while they are out doing dirty work.

    Hitman

    Description:

    • Kills people for money.
    • People can request a hit by pressing E on the Hitman.
    • Use /hitprice to change your price.

    Rules:

    • As a Hitman, you can only accept hits if there is a valid reason for it. Eg: Your client may have had his gun shop fired upon by a bandit.
    • As a Hitman, you cannot steal any valuables from any property they break into in order to complete a hit.
    • As a Hitman, you can only accept hits through the hit menu system.
    • As a Hitman, you are allowed to break into your targets property to complete a hit. (You do not have to advert this.)
    • Once you've completed (or failed) your hit, you must state the reason in/OOC. Eg: "// Hit complete. Reason: Breaking into clients' property."

    Tips:

    • Examples of legitimate hit reasons would be 'the target raided my base', 'the target kidnapped my boss' or 'the target killed my partner'.
    • Examples of illegitimate hit reasons would be 'the target is ugly' or 'the targets radio music sucks'.
    • You must know the reason why you are undertaking a hit and the reason must be roleplay based.

    Hobo

    Description:

    • The lowest member of society.
    • Build a shack on footpaths and such.
    • Search dumpsters for items.
    • Beg for food and/or money.

    Rules:

    • As a Hobo, you are not allowed to buy or own guns.
    • As a Hobo, you are not allowed to order hits, etc.
    • As a Hobo, you are not allowed to have doors.
    • As a Hobo, you are only allowed items you find legally.
    • As a Hobo, if you are arrested you may pay bail.
    • A hobo can defend another hobo provided that hobos life is in direct danger.
      • The above rule is a new addition. Players abusing this rule and baiting others into mass combat will be removed from the game.
    • As a Hobo, by roleplay you have no money.
      • Purchasing of any goods or trading with expenditure of money is FailRP.

    Tips:

    • Try setting up a little house and ask for donations.

    Junkie

    Description:

    • A lowlife addict.
    • Can make cocaine from his own home to either sell or fuel their addiction.

    Rules:

    • Can only base with one goon.
    • As the Mayor, keep the laws realistic and roleplay focused.

    Tips:

    • Use a cocaine barrel to view ingredients needed.
    • Use coca leaves are obtained from the coca plant.
    • Use sell your cocaine at drug dealers found around the map.

    Mayor

    Description:

    • Create laws and command the Police Chief.
    • Can give people Gun Licenses for a certain cost.
    • In times of chaos, the Mayor can initiate a lockdown with /lockdown.

    Rules:

    • As the Mayor, before initiating a lockdown you must /broadcast the reason. Eg: /broadcast Lockdown Reason: Gang violence on the street.
    • As the Mayor, keep the laws realistic and roleplay focused.
    • As the Mayor, the laws you place must not breach default laws or server rules. Eg: Arresting anyone with the name 'Johnny' on sight.
    • As the Mayor, you cannot act as a Police Officer or Crusader. You may only unarrest people.
    • As the Mayor, you cannot participate in raids or defend the PD unless your life is immediately threatened.
    • As the Mayor, you cannot make a law prohibiting jaywalking.
    • As you are a Government job, you may only 'base' in the PD. Government jobs cannot have private bases.
      • An 'outpost' (eg: rooftop pillbox) is not included as a base, but only S.W.A.T. can base within any 'outpost'.

    Tips:

    • Use /addlaw to make new laws.
    • Use /placelaws to make a lawboard.
    • Use /removelaw # to remove a law. (# being the law number you wish to remove)

    Meth Cook

    Description:

    • Set up a meth lab.
    • Cook drugs and sell them for cash.
    • The return rate on this job depends on your speed and skills.

    Tips:

    • Pocket your meth and take it to a drug dealer (found around the map) to sell it.
    • Hire a goon to protect your base for you.
    • You may only base with one goon. No other jobs.
    • Any visible meth equipment can be treated as such without needing to have direct line of sight proof.

    Police Officer/SWAT

    Description:

    • This job is not recommended for brand new players.
    • Patrol the streets and enforce the laws provided by the Mayor.
    • Protect citizens from criminals and danger.
    • Respond to police requests in the /cr channel.

    Rules:

    • As a Police Officer, you should always act in a manner that real life police do. Use reason, do not do something you wouldn't see in real life.
    • As a Police Officer, you may only shoot a criminal under the conditions that an RP life is directly in danger.
      • In all other situations you are required to use other methods such as talking, cuffing, searching and arresting.
    • As a Police Officer, do not act purely on word of mouth. Treat reports as just reports until you see otherwise with your own eyes.
    • As a Police Officer, do not random weapon check. You must have valid reason to search someone. Eg: If they wish to enter the PD or visit the Mayor.
    • As a Police Officer, do not stunstick spam or taser abuse, use these weapons for Law Enforcement purposes only.
    • As a Police Officer, do not random arrest/warrant/want; you must have a valid reason for these actions. Eg: Murder or Kidnap
    • As a Police Officer, offering bail is encouraged but not required. Bail cannot exceed $1000.
      • Once a criminal has been arrested, only the arresting officer or the Mayor can offer bail.
    • As a Police Officer, you may only raid a bank to arrest a wanted/warranted individual, or to obtain illegal goods such as weapons or drugs.
      • Money printers can be legally stored in banks, so these cannot be touched.
    • As a Police Officer, you may be corrupt. Eg: Occasionally letting someone pay for you to look the other way.
      • You can be arrested (and job demoted) if you are caught being corrupt by your colleagues.
    • As you are a Government job, you may only 'base' in the PD. Government jobs cannot have private bases.
      • An 'outpost' (eg: rooftop pillbox) is not included as a base, but only S.W.A.T. can base within any 'outpost'.

    Tips:

    • Any person with a weapon out on the street should be arrested on sight, regardless of licensing. There is no reason to have a gun out on the street.
    • Police Officers should only unholster their weapons in life-threatening circumstances. Non life-threatening situations should be approached with the taser.

    Police Chief

    Description:

    • The Chief is the leader of the Police and SWAT.
    • Coordinate the police forces to bring law to the city.

    Rules:

    • As the Police Chief, you cannot sell your job specific items to the general public, or to police at high profit.
    • Note: Police Officer rules also apply to this role. Click here to see them.

    Thief

    Description:

    • Break into shops and houses and steal things.
    • Pickpocket people for some extra cash.
    • This is a solo job, however they are allowed to base and raid with one other thief.
    • If raiding with another thief, you must /ooc Raid with 'FriendsName' or your partner must /ooc Raid Assist

    Rules:

    • As a Thief, you must /advert Raid to begin, and /advert Raid Over when complete.
    • As a Thief, you must wait 10 seconds after advertising to commence your raid.
    • As a Thief, you may live and work with one other thief.
      • If raiding together, you must /advert Raid and /advert Raid over togther.
